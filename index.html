Bearsharktopus Studios

Limited Liability Company

Bearsharktopus Studios provides web development, hosting, and ongoing technical administration for independently operated web platforms.

The sites we build and maintain are free for end users. Site administrators contract with us for development, infrastructure, and continued upkeep. Users who wish to support the ongoing operation of a platform may contribute through on-site support links — these contributions are processed on behalf of the site operator to cover the cost of hosting and administration services.

Services
  • Web Development
  • Site Administration
  • Hosting & Infrastructure
  • Platform Maintenance
  • Technical Consulting

All transactions processed through our payment provider reflect fees for services rendered or user contributions toward platform operating costs.
